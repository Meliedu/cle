Meli · HKUST Language Learning Platform

Work History & Forward Roadmap · 30 Mar – 31 Aug 2026

Design and engineering record for Meli, an AI-powered language learning platform for HKUST built on FastAPI, Next.js 16, pgvector and Cloudflare R2. The document covers three sections: (1) Weekly Summary of work shipped 30 Mar – 9 May 2026, (2) Forward Roadmap of eight 2-week sprints planned 11 May – 31 Aug 2026, and (3) a Dev Budget Estimate for the same forward period. A separate launch budget will be authored for September. Period total to date: ~346 non-merge commits.

Forward Roadmap · 11 May – 31 Aug 2026

The next ~16 weeks are organized into eight 2-week sprints, executed at a realistic solo-dev pace (~1 major theme per sprint plus polish). Mobile, production observability, and outbound email / SMS delivery infrastructure are explicitly deferred to the post-Aug launch budget, not silently dropped. Continuous tracks (research, dependency upkeep, Canvas sync robustness, cost monitoring) run in parallel.
